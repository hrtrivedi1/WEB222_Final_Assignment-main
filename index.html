CHESS - The Game of Strategy

What is chess?

Chess's roots can be traced back to India, where it was developed from the game of "chaturanga" before the seventh century. The game first became popular among European nations in the 9th century, thanks to Arabia and Persia. The contemporary powers of both the queen and the bishop were established in Spain in the final decades of the 15th century. The standardization of the game's current regulations occurred in the 19th century.
